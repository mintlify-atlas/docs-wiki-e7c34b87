This page defines the vocabulary used across the Apache Fineract internal docs. Each entry references at least one source file under `/home/daytona/repos/apache/fineract/` so engineers and coding agents can jump straight from the term to the type. Terms are grouped by area; everywhere a term has its own subsystem page (e.g. [`/cob/overview`](/cob/overview)), that page is linked.

## Money and accounting

### GL account

A general-ledger account in the Fineract chart of accounts. Modelled by `GLAccount` in `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/accounting/glaccount/domain/GLAccount.java` with classification by `GLAccountType` (asset, liability, equity, income, expense) and `GLAccountUsage` (header vs detail). Product-to-account mapping lives under `fineract-accounting/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/accounting/producttoaccountmapping/`.

### Journal entry

A double-entry bookkeeping record. Persisted as `JournalEntry` in `fineract-accounting/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/accounting/journalentry/domain/JournalEntry.java`. Each row carries the office, GL account, debit/credit amount, transaction reference and the originating product. Posting and reversal logic lives in `accounting/journalentry/service/`. See [`/accounting/overview`](/accounting/overview).

### Accrual

The process of recognising interest, fee or penalty income before cash settlement. Jobs and tasklets live under `fineract-accounting/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/accounting/accrual/` and `fineract-provider/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/accounting/accrual/` (job orchestration). Triggered as a COB business step on each loan.

### Trial balance

Per-period balance snapshot for every GL account in an office. Implemented under `fineract-accounting/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/accounting/trialbalance/`.

### Charge

A configurable fee or penalty applicable to a loan, savings or client. Modelled by `Charge` in `fineract-charge/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/portfolio/charge/domain/Charge.java`. Charges carry a `ChargeAppliesTo`, `ChargeTimeType` and `ChargeCalculationType`. Loan-specific charge plumbing lives in `fineract-loan/.../portfolio/loanaccount/`. See [`/charge/overview`](/charge/overview).

## Loan domain

### Loan

Classic loan account aggregate root: `Loan` under `fineract-loan/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/portfolio/loanaccount/domain/Loan.java`. Drives its own repayment schedule, transactions and accounting events. See [`/loan/overview`](/loan/overview).

### Progressive loan

A newer loan engine that emits incremental period adjustments rather than re-generating the full schedule on every event. Schedule generation lives in `fineract-progressive-loan/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/portfolio/loanaccount/loanschedule/domain/ProgressiveLoanScheduleGenerator.java`; the per-account model is `ProgressiveLoanModel` in `.../portfolio/loanaccount/domain/`. The schedule generator can be embedded standalone via `fineract-progressive-loan-embeddable-schedule-generator/`. See [`/progressive-loan/overview`](/progressive-loan/overview).

### Working capital loan

A revolving-credit loan variant that draws and repays inside a credit limit. Module: `fineract-working-capital-loan`. The COB lock entity is `WorkingCapitalLoanAccountLock` at `fineract-working-capital-loan/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/cob/domain/WorkingCapitalLoanAccountLock.java`; product entity under `portfolio/workingcapitalloanproduct/`. See [`/working-capital-loan/overview`](/working-capital-loan/overview).

### Delinquency

The set of rules and computed state that classify a loan as past due. Bucket and band entities live in `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/portfolio/delinquency/domain/` (`DelinquencyBucket`, `DelinquencyBucketType`, `DelinquencyMinimumPaymentPeriodAndRule`, `DelinquencyMinimumPaymentType`). Loan-side and progressive-loan-side computations override these in `fineract-loan/.../portfolio/delinquency/` and `fineract-progressive-loan/.../portfolio/delinquency/`. See [`/loan/overview`](/loan/overview) for how delinquency interacts with COB.

### GLIM (Group Loan Individual Monitoring)

A group loan whose individual members each retain a member balance and repayment obligation. Bulk repayments and approvals are handled by GLIM-specific command handlers under `fineract-loan/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/portfolio/loanaccount/handler/` — e.g. `GLIMBulkRepaymentCommandHandler`, `GLIMApplicationRejectionCommandHandler`, `GLIMLoanApplicationApprovalCommandHandler`, `GlimLoanApplicationDisburseCommandHandler`, `UndoGLIMLoanApplicationApproval`. The container DTO is `GLIMContainer` in `fineract-loan/.../portfolio/loanaccount/data/GLIMContainer.java`; the per-member info repository is `GLIMAccountInfoRepository`.

### GSIM (Group Savings Individual Monitoring)

The savings analogue of GLIM. Handlers live under `fineract-provider/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/portfolio/savings/handler/` — `GSIMApplicationSubmittalCommandHandler`, `GSIMApplicationApprovalCommandHandler`, `GSIMApplicationModificationCommandHandler`, `GSIMApplicationRejectionHandler`, `GSIMAccountActivationCommandHandler`, `CloseGSIMCommandHandler`. A missing GSIM member raises `GroupMemberNotFoundInGSIMException` in `fineract-provider/.../portfolio/group/exception/`.

## Organisation

### Tenant

A Fineract tenant is a logical customer with its own database schema, accessed via the `Fineract-Platform-TenantId` HTTP header. Resolved by `TenantDetailsService` (`fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/infrastructure/core/service/tenant/TenantDetailsService.java`) and pinned to the current thread by `ThreadLocalContextUtil`. Connection details (including optional read-only mirror) are held in `FineractPlatformTenantConnection` at `fineract-core/.../infrastructure/core/domain/`. Per-tenant DataSources are built by `DataSourcePerTenantServiceFactory` (`fineract-core/.../infrastructure/core/service/database/`). See [`/core/overview`](/core/overview).

### Office

A branch or hierarchy node within a tenant. Entity `Office`; data DTO `OfficeData` in `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/organisation/office/data/OfficeData.java`. Offices form a parent-child hierarchy used for permissions and reporting.

### Staff

A staff member attached to an office. Entity `Staff` in `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/organisation/staff/domain/Staff.java`, enums in `StaffEnumerations.java`.

### Holiday

A non-business day declared at the office level. Entity `Holiday` in `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/organisation/holiday/domain/Holiday.java`; status enum in `HolidayStatusType`. API constants in `fineract-core/.../organisation/holiday/api/HolidayApiConstants.java`. Influences repayment date rolling on COB.

### Working day

Configuration of which days of the week are business days, plus repayment-rescheduling rules for non-working days. Entity `WorkingDays` in `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/organisation/workingdays/domain/WorkingDays.java`; enumerations in `WorkingDaysEnumerations.java`; constants in `WorkingDaysApiConstants.java`.

### Fund

A funding source attached to a loan or loan product. Entity `Fund` in `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/portfolio/fund/domain/Fund.java`; repository `FundRepository`; DTO `FundData` in `.../fund/data/`.

### Branch

A teller/cashier-aware view of an office, owned by module `fineract-branch`. Teller, cashier and cash-management entities live under `fineract-branch/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/organisation/teller/`.

## Command and audit infrastructure

### Command

A maker-side mutating intent (`POST/PUT/DELETE`) translated into a `CommandWrapper` by `CommandWrapperBuilder` (`fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/commands/service/CommandWrapperBuilder.java`). The wrapper carries `entityName`, `actionName`, JSON body, locale and dates. Discovered handlers implement `NewCommandSourceHandler` (`fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/commands/handler/NewCommandSourceHandler.java`) and are annotated with `@CommandType(entity = "...", action = "...")` from `fineract-core/.../commands/annotation/CommandType.java`. See [`/command/overview`](/command/overview).

### CommandSource

The audit row written for each processed command. Entity `CommandSource` at `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/commands/domain/CommandSource.java`; repository `CommandSourceRepository`. Status values come from `CommandProcessingResultType` (`AWAITING_APPROVAL`, `PROCESSED`, `REJECTED`, `UNDER_PROCESSING`, `ERROR`).

### Command wrapper

The in-memory DTO produced by `CommandWrapperBuilder.build()`. Type: `CommandWrapper` at `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/commands/domain/CommandWrapper.java`. The wrapper drives both `SynchronousCommandProcessingService.executeCommand(...)` and the maker-checker queue: `PortfolioCommandSourceWritePlatformServiceImpl` (`fineract-core/.../commands/service/PortfolioCommandSourceWritePlatformServiceImpl.java`).

### Maker-checker

A four-eyes workflow where a maker enqueues a command and a separate checker approves it. Enforced via the `command_source.checker_id` column. Configuration: `MakerCheckerRequest` DTO at `fineract-provider/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/commands/data/request/MakerCheckerRequest.java`; checker permissions managed by `UpdateMakerCheckerPermissionsCommandHandler` at `fineract-provider/.../useradministration/handler/`. Errors include `CommandNotAwaitingApprovalException` and `RollbackTransactionNotApprovedException` under `fineract-core/.../commands/exception/`.

### Idempotency key

A client-supplied header that uniquely identifies a write request so a retry returns the original result. Header name is configurable via `fineract.idempotency-key-header-name` (default `Idempotency-Key`) in `application.properties`. Resolution is performed by `IdempotencyKeyResolver` at `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/commands/service/IdempotencyKeyResolver.java`; absent keys are synthesised by `IdempotencyKeyGenerator` in the same package. The resolved key is stored on `CommandSource` and consulted by `SynchronousCommandProcessingService` on retries.

## COB (Close of Business)

### COB

The Close-of-Business pipeline that advances each loan to the next business day. Lives in module `fineract-cob`. The principal entry point is the `LOAN_COB` partitioned job (configured by `fineract.partitioned-job.partitioned-job-properties[0]` in `application.properties`). Implementation: `fineract-cob/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/cob/`; loan-specific steps in `fineract-loan/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/cob/loan/`; savings steps in `fineract-savings/.../cob/savings/`; investor steps in `fineract-investor/.../investor/cob/`; working-capital steps in `fineract-working-capital-loan/.../cob/workingcapitalloan/`. See [`/cob/overview`](/cob/overview).

### BusinessStep

A pluggable unit of COB work. Contract: `COBBusinessStep` at `fineract-cob/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/cob/COBBusinessStep.java`; runtime service `COBBusinessStepService` and `COBBusinessStepServiceImpl` in the same package. Configuration DTO `BusinessStep` lives at `fineract-cob/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/cob/data/BusinessStep.java`; the full per-job ordering is held in `JobBusinessStepConfigData`. Each step gets the current `BusinessDate` from `BusinessDateResolver` (`fineract-cob/.../cob/resolver/BusinessDateResolver.java`).

### Business-date

A per-tenant date that COB advances after every successful run; distinct from the system clock. Entity and API: `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/infrastructure/businessdate/` (`BusinessDateApiResource`, `BusinessDateUpdateCommand`, `BusinessDateResponse`, `BusinessDateUpdateRequest`). Multiple `BusinessDateType`s exist; the current map is held by `ThreadLocalContextUtil.setBusinessDates(...)` / `getBusinessDate(...)` and surfaced to COB steps via `BusinessDateResolver`.

## Eventing

### Business event

An internal Spring-`ApplicationEvent`-style notification raised by domain services and consumed by listeners in the same JVM. Contract under `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/infrastructure/event/business/`. Used widely by accounting and accrual code.

### External event

A serialisable event emitted outside the JVM, gated by `fineract.events.external.enabled` in `application.properties` and Avro-typed under `fineract-avro-schemas/src/main/avro/`. Persistence row: `ExternalEvent` at `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/infrastructure/event/external/repository/domain/ExternalEvent.java`. Producers (JMS, Kafka) live in `fineract-provider/.../infrastructure/event/external/producer/`. The `NoExternalEvent` marker class at `fineract-core/.../event/business/domain/NoExternalEvent.java` is used to opt a business event out of external emission. Conditions: `EnableExternalEventQueueCondition`, `EnableExternalEventTopicCondition` under `.../event/external/config/`.

## Datatables and code values

### Datatable

An optional, per-tenant SQL table associated with a system entity (`m_client`, `m_loan`, etc.) — Fineract's user-driven schema extension mechanism. Validator: `DataTableValidator` at `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/infrastructure/dataqueries/data/DataTableValidator.java`. Datatable status DTOs: `DatatableChecksData`, `DatatableCheckStatusData`. API constants: `DataTableApiConstant`. Maker-checker integration uses `DatatableCommandFromApiJsonDeserializer` in `fineract-core/.../infrastructure/core/serialization/`.

### Code value

An entry of an extensible lookup list (gender, payment type, document type, etc.). Entity `CodeValue` at `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/infrastructure/codes/domain/CodeValue.java`; DTO `CodeValueData`; REST API `CodeValuesApiResourceSwagger`. Code values are referenced from many domain entities by `code_value_id`.

## Extended definitions

The terms below recur in technical discussions but did not warrant a stand-alone section above.

### Tenant master vs tenant schema

Fineract uses a two-tier database model: a single **tenant master** schema (configured by `fineract.tenant.*` keys in `application.properties`) stores `tenants` and `tenant_server_connections`, while each tenant has its own **tenant schema** that holds business data. The master is opened in `JdbcConfig` (`fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/infrastructure/core/config/JdbcConfig.java`); per-tenant pools are built lazily by `DataSourcePerTenantServiceFactory.createNewDataSourceFor(...)`.

### Read-only DataSource

When a node reports `fineractProperties.getMode().isReadOnlyMode() == true`, the tenant connection record's `read_only_*` fields (host, port, name, credentials) are substituted at pool-build time and `HikariConfig.setReadOnly(true)` is called. See [`/overview/runtime-modes`](/overview/runtime-modes) for the full table.

### Resilience4j retry

Wrappers around `SynchronousCommandProcessingService` use Resilience4j (`io.github.resilience4j:resilience4j-spring-boot3`, declared in `fineract-core/dependencies.gradle`). The per-command retry policy is built by `RetryConfigurationAssembler` at `fineract-core/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/commands/configuration/RetryConfigurationAssembler.java`.

### Stuck job

A Spring Batch job that has been in `STARTED` state longer than `fineract.job.stuck-retry-threshold` (default 5 retries). Detected and re-launched by `StuckJobListener` at `fineract-provider/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/infrastructure/jobs/service/StuckJobListener.java` — only loaded when `fineract.mode.batch-manager-enabled=true`.

### Partitioned job

A Spring Batch job that splits work into partitions executed in parallel. The principal Fineract example is `LOAN_COB`, configured by `fineract.partitioned-job.partitioned-job-properties[0]` (chunk size, partition size, thread pool sizing, retry limit, poll interval) in `application.properties`. Manager-side wiring: `fineract-provider/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/infrastructure/springbatch/ManagerConfig.java`. Worker-side wiring: `WorkerConfig.java` in the same package.

### Remote partitioning transport

Spring Events, JMS or Kafka. Selected by `fineract.remote-job-message-handler.spring-events.enabled`, `.jms.enabled`, `.kafka.enabled`. Per-transport conditions live under `fineract-provider/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/infrastructure/springbatch/messagehandler/conditions/`.

### Tenant timezone

Each tenant carries a `Asia/Kolkata`-style ZoneId (default from `fineract.tenant.timezone`). Used by `ThreadLocalContextUtil` when materialising `LocalDate`/`OffsetDateTime` from the JVM clock.

### Loan transaction processor

A strategy that decides how a loan repayment is allocated across principal, interest, fees and penalties. Enabled processors are listed in `application.properties` under `fineract.loan.transactionprocessor.*` keys; in-tree implementations include `creocore`, `early-repayment`, `mifos-standard`, `heavensfamily`, `interest-principal-penalties-fees`, `principal-interest-penalties-fees`, `rbi-india`, the long-named due/penalty variants, and `advanced-payment-strategy`. The default behaviour when a processor isn't found is controlled by `fineract.loan.transactionprocessor.error-not-found-fail`.

### Content store

The pluggable backend for document attachments. Toggled by `fineract.content.filesystem.enabled` (default `true`, root `${user.home}/.fineract`) and `fineract.content.s3.enabled`. Implementations live in `fineract-document/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/infrastructure/contentstore/`. Filename and MIME whitelists are enforced via `fineract.content.regex-whitelist` and `fineract.content.mime-whitelist`.

### Correlation ID

An HTTP header (default `X-Correlation-ID`) that Fineract propagates into the MDC for log correlation. Enabled via `fineract.correlation.enabled` in `application.properties`.

### MIX taxonomy

The MIX Market XBRL taxonomy that Fineract can emit for regulatory reporting. Lives in module `fineract-mix`; `mix/api/` exposes REST resources and `mix/mapping/` performs the XBRL generation.

### Interoperation (Mojaloop)

The Mojaloop / Open API for FSP Interoperability endpoint set. Implementation: `fineract-savings/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/interoperation/` and `fineract-provider/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/interoperation/`.

### SQL-injection regex profile

Eight regex rules (`fineract.sql-validation.patterns[0..7]` — inject-blind, detect-entry-point, inject-timing, detect-backend, detect-column, detect-out-of-bands, inject-stacked-query, inject-comment) grouped into two profiles (`main`, `adhoc`). Definitions in `application.properties`; enforcement in `fineract-security/src/main/java/org/apache/fineract/infrastructure/security/`. The PostgreSQL driver is included specifically for SQL-injection prevention utilities — see `fineract-security/dependencies.gradle`.

### Custom module

A Gradle subproject under `custom/<company>/<category>/<module>` automatically picked up by the loader at the bottom of `settings.gradle`. The reference vendor is `acme` (`custom/acme/`); see [`/overview/module-graph`](/overview/module-graph) for the loader rules and the full list of `acme` modules.
